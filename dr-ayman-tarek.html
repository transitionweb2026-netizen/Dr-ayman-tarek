About Dr. Ayman Tarek

Dr. Ayman Tarek is a Consultant Plastic, Cosmetic & Reconstructive Surgeon with more than 15 years of experience. His approach combines internationally recognized surgical techniques with meticulous attention to detail, tailored to each patient's unique goals.

He holds several internationally accredited fellowships and has presented at dozens of scientific conferences worldwide, with an unwavering commitment to safety and quality at every stage of the patient journey.
